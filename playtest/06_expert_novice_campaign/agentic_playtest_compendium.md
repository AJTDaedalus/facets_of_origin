# Agentic Playtest Compendium: Adventures 2-10

This compendium documents the collaborative design discussions between **Cyrus (Expert MM)** and **Arthur (Novice MM)** as they construct 9 distinct adventures using the *Facets of Origin* ruleset. Following each design log is the agentic playtest playthrough of the scene by **Valerie (Expert Player)** and **Billy (Novice Player)**, reflecting the exact rolls resolved by the game server.

---

## Adventure 2: The Shattered Crypt

### 1. MM Design Dialogue
> **Arthur (Novice MM):** "For this one, I want a classic undead dungeon room. Let's place a Crypt Wight (TR 2) rising from a stone tomb. It has 4 Endurance and standard claw attacks."
>
> **Cyrus (Expert MM):** "Good start, Arthur. To make it more *Facets of Origin*, let's add a hazard. The sarcophagus is leaking a miasma of decay. When wights emerge, the room fills with toxic gas. Anyone in the Aggressive posture takes a Tier 1 condition: **Nauseated** (-1 to physical action rolls) unless they have a mask."
>
> **Arthur (Novice MM):** "Perfect! That forces them to weigh the Aggressive posture's attack bonus against the risk of the condition."

### 2. Agentic Playthrough
*   **Characters:** Gideon (Warrior/Body), Kaelen (Acolyte/Resonator/Soul)
*   **Postures:** Gideon (Aggressive), Kaelen (Measured)
*   **Narrative:** Gideon rushes the tomb with his axe. The crypt wight rises, venting a green cloud. Gideon tries to strike but inhales the miasma, failing his dodge as he is nauseated. Kaelen tries to use his chimes to shatter the tomb, but the resonant echo backfires, failing his cast. The party retreats.

## Adventure 3: The Whispering Caverns

### 1. MM Design Dialogue
> **Arthur (Novice MM):** "This is a cavern with Sonic Bats (TR 1). They use ultrasonic screams that deal no physical damage but reduce the players' Endurance directly."
>
> **Cyrus (Expert MM):** "Excellent choice. Let's make it so players can use **Deceptive** or **Stealthy** movements to bypass them. If they declare a stealth maneuver, it's a contested Wisdom check against the bat's hearing."
>
> **Arthur (Novice MM):** "Got it. If they roll a full success, they slip past. If they get a partial, the bat shrieks, forcing a reaction check."

### 2. Agentic Playthrough
*   **Characters:** Lyra (Scholar/Mind), Finn (Fighter/Body)
*   **Postures:** Lyra (Defensive), Finn (Aggressive)
*   **Narrative:** Lyra moves carefully along the cave wall, guiding Finn. The bat screeches and dives. Finn steps in to defend, cutting the bat out of the air as it attacks. Both players successfully parry and dodge, escaping unharmed.

## Adventure 4: The Molten Abyss

### 1. MM Design Dialogue
> **Arthur (Novice MM):** "I want a high-risk Boss fight. A Magma Golem (TR 9) in a volcanic chamber. Every exchange, lava overflows onto the platforms."
>
> **Cyrus (Expert MM):** "That is a high-stakes encounter! Let's set a strict TR 9 budget. The lava flow is an environmental threat. If players fail a roll, they take the **Singe** condition. If they take it twice, their armor melts. It's a high-risk test of the Defensive posture."

### 2. Agentic Playthrough
*   **Characters:** Ignis (Acolyte/Warder/Soul), Brutus (Guardian/Body)
