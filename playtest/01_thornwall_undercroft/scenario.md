# Scenario: The Thornwall Undercroft

## MM's Prep Notes (Jamie)

> I'm building around the established lore — Thornwall has a Municipal Archive, and the PHB examples reference it. I'll set the adventure in the restricted lower levels. The three-act structure from MM2 gives me a framework: Hook → Rising Action → Climax/Cliffhanger.

### Campaign Pitch (One Paragraph)
The Thornwall Municipal Archive has a problem. The lower stacks — sealed for decades — have been making noises. The head archivist, Alderman Brost, wants discreet investigators, not the Watch (who would shut down the whole building). The party was recommended by a mutual contact. The job: go down, find out what's happening, stop it if possible. Payment: 50 silver and a favor from the Archive.

### Session Structure

**Act I — The Hook (15 min):**
The party meets Alderman Brost in his office above the Archive. He's nervous. Strange sounds from below, a clerk who went down and came back shaking. Social scene: get the brief, ask questions, negotiate.

**Act II — Rising Action (60 min):**
- **Scene 1: The Upper Stacks** — Exploration. Dim corridors, old records, environmental storytelling. Investigation checks to find clues about what's below.
- **Scene 2: The Sealed Door** — A warded door to the lower stacks. Needs to be opened — physical force, magical inspection, or the key (which Brost gave them, but it's corroded and the lock is jammed).
- **Scene 3: The Lower Stacks** — Deeper exploration. The architecture changes. Ward-lanterns flicker. Something is moving in the dust.
- **Scene 4: The Mook Ambush** — Dust constructs (3 Mooks). A Skirmish encounter to introduce combat mechanics. Tests postures, strikes, reactions.

**Act III — Climax (30 min):**
- **Scene 5: The Guardian Chamber** — The Archive Guardian, an ancient construct protecting a sealed vault. Named NPC encounter (Standard difficulty). Has a weakness (can be deactivated with the right knowledge/magic) and a phase change (when Staggered, activates defensive wards).
- **Scene 6: The Vault** — What was it guarding? A collection of sealed documents — contracts, debts, blackmail material from a century ago. The real question: what does Brost want with these? Cliffhanger ending.

### NPCs

**Alderman Brost** (Social)
- **Want:** The sealed documents in the vault (they contain proof of his family's historical land claims)
- **Secret:** He knows exactly what's down there. The "strange noises" are real but he's been waiting for the right excuse to send people in.
- **Connection:** Kael's old Watch captain mentioned Brost as "a man who keeps his word but chooses his words carefully"
- **Detail:** Speaks with his hands folded precisely on the desk, never gestures

**Lira** (One-shot NPC, the shaken clerk)
- **Detail:** Young, still rattled, keeps touching the back of her neck where something cold brushed her
- **Info:** "It wasn't an animal. It moved like purpose. Like it was looking for something that wasn't me."

### Enemies

**Dust Construct (Mook)** — TR 1
- Attack modifier: +0 (Offense value: 2)
- No Endurance (Mook: one Strike removes)
- No armor
- Description: Humanoid shapes made of compressed dust and old paper, animated by residual ward magic. Move silently, strike with hardened limbs.
- TR: Offense 2 + Durability 0 = 2. As Mooks, minimum TR 1. Using TR 1 each.

**The Archive Guardian (Named NPC)** — TR 8
- Tier: Named NPC
- Endurance: 6 (Durability value: 3)
- Attack modifier: +1 (Offense value: 3)
- Defense modifier: +0
- Armor: Light (Armor bonus: 1)
- Technique: Defensive Ward — once per exchange, can force one attacker to re-roll (Technique bonus: 1)
- TR: 3 + 3 + 1 + 1 = **8**
- Phase Change: When first Staggered, activates a shimmering ward field — all attacks against it become Hard difficulty until the ward is disrupted (by magical means or targeting the ward-lantern overhead).
- Personality: Not malevolent. Patient. Doing its job. Will give verbal warning before attacking: "Authorized personnel only. Please present credentials."
- Lateral Solutions:
  - Vessa's Inscription domain can identify and disrupt the ward glyphs on its body (Investigate or Lore + magic)
  - Mira's Resonance can detect its harmonic frequency and disrupt it (Attune + magic)
  - The ward-lantern overhead powers the constructs; extinguishing it weakens the Guardian (but darkness)
  - Kael's Watch Specialty: he knows about old city constructs from patrol briefings — "Standard decommission phrase is part of the ward inscription"

### Encounter Budget Check

**Party Strength:** 3 (each character has 1 career advance)

**Scene 4 — Dust Construct Ambush:**
- 3 Dust Constructs × TR 1 = TR 3
- 2-3 enemies: ×1.0 action economy multiplier
- Effective TR: 3
- Budget: Skirmish (3 × 1 = 3). Matches exactly. ~95% win rate.

**Scene 5 — Archive Guardian:**
- 1 Guardian = TR 8
- Solo enemy: ×0.75 action economy multiplier
- Effective TR: 6
- Budget: Standard (3 × 2 = 6). Matches. ~75% win rate.
- With lateral solutions available, actual difficulty is lower — party can shortcut the fight.
