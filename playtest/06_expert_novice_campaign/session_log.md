## Act I: The Wet Gate

**Cyrus (MM):** Your small rowboat bumps gently against the ancient, slime-slick stone archway of the Sunken Spire of Aethelgard. Water from the Lake of Sorrows laps at the rusted bronze gate that blocks the entrance. The gate is massive, locked by a central disk carved with ancient Weaver glyphs. Behind it, you can hear the deep, metallic echo of grinding gears. What do you do?

> **Alaric:** I'll steady the boat against the stone arch and inspect the gate. "Pippa, hand me the lantern. Let's see if this lock is mechanical or something worse."
>
> **Sylvia:** "Keep the light steady, Alaric. The Weaver glyphs on the lock might still hold active wards. Let me examine the rune patterns."
>
> **Pippa:** "I don't like sitting in a wet boat. Can I just try to pick the lock? I've got my lockpicks from Oakhaven."
>
> **Thorne:** "I... I want to listen. Maybe the sounds of the gears have a pattern? Or a spiritual echo?"

### [Mechanical Resolution: Act I Gates]

**Cyrus (MM):** Let's resolve these actions. 
* **Sylvia**, you are studying the runes. That's a **Lore** roll (Mind-focused). It's a **Standard** difficulty check (+0).
* **Pippa**, picking the lock is a **Finesse** roll (Dexterity + Finesse skill). You are Novice in Finesse (+0), but you have Dexterity 3 (+1 modifier). So that's 2d6+1. This is a **Hard** lock because of the rust (-1 difficulty modifier), so effectively you roll 2d6+0.
* **Thorne**, listening for spiritual echoes is an **Attune** roll (Soul-focused). You have Spirit 3 (+1) and are Practiced in Attune (+1). That's 2d6+2 at **Standard** difficulty.

**Sophia (Sylvia):** I'll roll Lore. My modifier is +1 (Lore Practiced) +1 (Knowledge modifier) = +2.
* *Roll:* 4 + 3 = 7 + 2 = **9**. A partial success.

**Cyrus (MM):** On a **7-9 (Partial Success)**, you decipher the glyphs, but it comes with a cost or limitation. You realize the glyphs represent a sealing sequence. You know how to unlock it, but doing so will drain some residual magic, causing the water level inside the gate-chamber to rise rapidly, trapping your boat. 

> **Sylvia:** "The runes are a seal. If we turn the mechanism, the inner chamber will flood further. We might lose the boat."

**Penny (Pippa):** I still want to try picking it so we don't have to trigger the magic! I'll roll Finesse. My modifier is +1 (Dex) +0 (Finesse skill Novice) -1 (Hard difficulty) = +0.
* *Roll:* 5 + 3 = 8. **8 (Partial Success)**.

**Cyrus (MM):** You open the lock mechanism, but because it's a partial success, your lockpick snaps in the rusted tumblers with a loud, metallic *CLANG* that echoes deep into the spire. The gate swings open, but you've lost your lockpicks, and whatever is inside now knows you are here.

> **Pippa:** "Oops... Well, the gate is open! But my favorite pick is broken."

**Toby (Thorne):** I want to roll Attune to see if my listening action detected the echo before the clang. Modifier is +1 (Spirit) +1 (Attune Practiced) = +2.
* *Roll:* 6 + 4 = 10 + 2 = **12**. A full success (10+).

**Cyrus (MM):** Excellent roll, Toby! Because you got a **10+ (Full Success)**, you tune out the scraping metal and hear the rhythm of the spire. You hear a low, rhythmic thrumming sound beneath the water. You recognize it as a harmonic cycle of the security sentinels. You gain a **+1 forward modifier** to your next action regarding the sentinels, as you can anticipate their movement patterns.

## Act II: The Flooded Scriptorium

**Cyrus (MM):** You push past the bronze gate. You are now inside the Flooded Scriptorium. The water is chest-deep, freezing cold, and filled with floating, decayed scrolls. Narrow, slick stone paths run like a maze above the water, but they are wet and unstable. 
Suddenly, the water bubbles. Four skeletal figures draped in rotting robes and carrying rusted iron spears rise from the dark water. Their eyes glow with faint blue light. They block your path.

### [Initiating Combat: Simultaneous Exchange 1]

**Luke (Alaric):** Alright, combat is simultaneous in *Facets of Origin*. There are no turns. We all declare our **postures** first. Toby, Penny, here’s how we do it:
* **Aggressive:** +1 to attacks, but reactions (like dodging) cost +1 Endurance.
* **Measured (Balanced):** Normal rolls, normal costs.
* **Defensive:** -1 to attacks, but reactions cost -1 Endurance (great for protecting others).
* **Withdrawn:** Can't attack, but reactions are free and you recover 2 Endurance.

**Luke (Alaric):** I'll declare **Defensive** posture. I want to stand at the front with my shield and mace to draw their attacks.
**Sophia (Sylvia):** I'll choose **Measured** posture. I want to cast an Inscription rune on the stone path to blast them.
**Penny (Pippa):** I'm going **Aggressive**! I want to jump off the path and stab them with my daggers!
**Toby (Thorne):** I'll go **Measured**. I want to try casting Resonance magic to shatter their spears.

**Cyrus (MM):** The 4 Water-Logged Sentinels (Mooks, TR 1 each) rise. Three of them lunge at Alaric on the path. One of them lunges at Pippa as she jumps into the water.

#### [Exchange 1 Resolution]

##### 1. Pippa's Attack & Defense (Aggressive Posture)
* **Action:** Pippa attacks Sentinel 1.
* **Roll:** Dexterity + Combat (Finesse used as combat modifier +1) + 1 (Aggressive Posture) = +2.
* *Roll:* 4 + 4 = 8 + 2 = **10**. Full Success!
* **Result:** Pippa strikes Sentinel 1. As a Mook (TR 1), a single strike destroys it. It crumbles into bones and sinks.
* **Defense:** Sentinel 1 lunged at Pippa before dying. Pippa must react. She chooses to **Dodge** (Dexterity roll).
* **Cost:** Dodge normally costs 1 Endurance. In Aggressive posture, it costs **2 Endurance**.
* **Roll:** Dexterity 3 (+1) + 0 (Stealth/Finesse modifier not applicable for generic dodge) = +1 modifier.
* *Roll:* 5 + 3 = 8 + 1 = **9**. Partial Success (7-9).
* **Result:** Pippa dodges the spear blow, but the partial success means she slips off the narrow path into the deep, freezing water. Moving back onto the path will now require an Athletics roll at Hard difficulty. Pippa reduces her Endurance pool by 2 (from 4 to **2**).

##### 2. Alaric's Attack & Defense (Defensive Posture)
* **Action:** Alaric attacks Sentinel 2 with his mace.
* **Roll:** Combat (+1) + Str (+1) - 1 (Defensive Posture) = +1 modifier.
* *Roll:* 6 + 3 = 9 + 1 = **10**. Full Success!
* **Result:** Alaric smashes Sentinel 2, destroying it instantly.
* **Defense:** Sentinels 3 and 4 attack Alaric. Alaric has to react to two attacks. He chooses to **Parry** Sentinel 3 and **Absorb** Sentinel 4.
* **Cost:** 
  - Parry costs 1 Endurance. In Defensive posture, it costs **0 Endurance**.
  - Absorb costs 0 Endurance (takes full effect, but downgraded by Armor).
* **Parry Roll (Sentinel 3):** Str (+1) + Combat (+1) = +2.
* *Roll:* 4 + 5 = 9 + 2 = **11**. Full Success (10+). No damage taken.
* **Absorb Result (Sentinel 4):** Sentinel 4 hits Alaric. A standard strike deals 2 damage. Alaric has **Light Armor (Tier 1)**, which downgrades the damage by 1. Alaric takes 1 damage to his Endurance pool (from 5 to **4**).

##### 3. Sylvia's Inscription Magic (Measured Posture)
* **Action:** Sylvia scribes a glyph of freezing force on the path in front of Sentinel 3.
* **Spell Scope:** Minor (Normally Easy, but pre-technique makes it **Standard** difficulty, +0 modifier).
* **Roll:** Knowledge (+1) + Lore (+1) = +2 modifier. Sophia spends 1 Spark to ensure success. She rolls 3d6, keeping the two highest.
* *Roll:* 6, 5, 2 (keep 6 and 5) = 11 + 2 = **13**. Full Success (10+).
* **Result:** The path freezes over in a flash. Sentinel 3 steps on it, is frozen solid, and crumbles. Only Sentinel 4 remains.

##### 4. Thorne's Resonance Magic (Measured Posture)
* **Action:** Thorne uses his +1 forward modifier from Act I to cast Resonance magic (vibrating the water to stagger the remaining Sentinel).
* **Spell Scope:** Minor (Normally Standard, but pre-technique makes it **Hard** difficulty, -1 modifier).
* **Roll:** Spirit (+1) + Attune (+1) - 1 (Hard) + 1 (Forward Modifier) = +2 modifier. Toby forgets to spend a Spark.
* *Roll:* 2 + 3 = 5 + 2 = **7**. Partial Success (7-9).
* **Result:** The water vibrates violently, disrupting Sentinel 4's posture and making all attacks against it Easy next exchange. However, the partial success means the feedback thrums through Thorne's staff. Thorne takes 1 damage to his Endurance pool (from 4 to **3**).

### [Simultaneous Exchange 2]

**Luke (Alaric):** Sentinel 4 is the only one left, and it's off-balance. Let's finish it.
* **Alaric:** Measured posture.
* **Sylvia:** Measured posture.
* **Thorne:** Withdrawn posture (to recover his lost Endurance).
* **Pippa:** Measured posture.

**Cyrus (MM):** Sentinel 4, off-balance, desperately lunges at the nearest target, which is Alaric.

#### [Exchange 2 Resolution]

##### 1. Pippa's Attack
* **Action:** Pippa flings a throwing knife from the water at Sentinel 4.
* **Roll:** Dexterity (+1) + Combat (+0) + 1 (Easy target status from Thorne's magic) = +2 modifier.
* *Roll:* 5 + 4 = 9 + 2 = **11**. Full Success!
* **Result:** The knife pierces the Sentinel's skull. It drops into the dark water, silent.

##### 2. Thorne's Recovery (Withdrawn Posture)
* **Result:** Thorne recovers 2 Endurance (pool goes from 3 back to **4**).

**Cyrus (MM):** The room falls silent. The immediate danger is gone, but Pippa is shivering in the deep, freezing water.

> **Alaric:** I lean over the stone path and offer my mace handle to Pippa. "Grab hold, Pippa! Let's pull you out of there before the cold sets in."
>
> **Pippa:** "Brr... Thanks, Alaric. The water is freezing!"

**Cyrus (MM):** Pippa, make a standard **Constitution + Athletics** check (Standard difficulty) to climb back onto the slippery path. 
* **Pippa:** My modifier is Con 2 (+0) + Athletics Novice (+0) = +0. I'll spend a Spark this time! (Rolls 3d6, keeps top 2).
* *Roll:* 5, 4, 1 (keep 5 and 4) = 9 + 0 = **9**. Partial Success (7-9).
* **Result:** You scramble back onto the path, but in your rush, your wet boot slips, and you drop your rusty shortsword into the deep water. It's lost in the dark mud below.

> **Pippa:** "Ugh, my sword! This spire really hates my gear."

## Act III: The Hearth-Core Vault

**Cyrus (MM):** You travel deeper, descending down stone steps until you enter a dry, octagonal chamber. In the center, a glowing red crystal—the Hearth-Core—pulses with intense heat, venting steam into the ceiling. Standing between you and the core is a 9-foot-tall construct made of granite slabs and bronze pipework. Its chest glows with matching steam valves. It raises its massive stone fists.
*"Intruders detected. Secure perimeter."*

### [Simultaneous Exchange 1 (Boss Fight)]

* **Alaric:** Defensive posture (he wants to absorb the golem's heavy blows).
* **Pippa:** Measured posture (only has throwing knives left).
* **Sylvia:** Measured posture (preparing a freezing glyph).
* **Thorne:** Measured posture (planning to disrupt the golem's steam pipes).

**Cyrus (MM):** The Sentry Golem (TR 9) sweeps its massive stone arm in a wide arc, targeting both Alaric and Pippa in close range. It also uses its **Steam Blast** technique to fill the room with blinding vapor.

#### [Exchange 1 Resolution]

##### 1. The Golem's Steam Blast
* **Effect:** Everyone must make a **Wisdom + Survival/Insight** check (Hard difficulty, -1) to avoid being blinded (giving a -1 penalty to all rolls next exchange).
* **Alaric Roll:** Wis 2 (+0) - 1 (Hard) = -1. 
  - *Roll:* 6 + 3 = 9 - 1 = **8** (Partial success: not blinded, but takes 1 Endurance damage from hot steam. Endurance goes from 4 to **3**).
* **Sylvia Roll:** Wis 2 (+0) - 1 (Hard) = -1.
  - *Roll:* 5 + 4 = 9 - 1 = **8** (Partial success: takes 1 Endurance damage. Endurance goes from 4 to **3**).
* **Thorne Roll:** Wis 2 (+0) - 1 (Hard) = -1.
  - *Roll:* 6 + 5 = 11 - 1 = **10** (Full success: avoids the steam blast entirely).
* **Pippa Roll:** Wis 2 (+0) - 1 (Hard) = -1. Penny spends a Spark! (Rolls 3d6, keeps top 2).
  - *Roll:* 6, 5, 2 (keep 6 and 5) = 11 - 1 = **10** (Full success: avoids the steam blast).

##### 2. Alaric's Defense & Attack (Defensive Posture)
* **Defense:** Golem attacks Alaric with a crushing swing (deals 3 damage). Alaric reacts with **Parry**.
* **Cost:** 0 Endurance (Defensive posture).
* **Roll:** Str (+1) + Combat (+1) = +2.
* *Roll:* 4 + 4 = 8 + 2 = **10** (Full success). The strike is blocked completely by Alaric's shield.
* **Attack:** Alaric strikes back.
* **Roll:** Str (+1) + Combat (+1) - 1 (Defensive) = +1 modifier.
* *Roll:* 5 + 3 = 8 + 1 = **9** (Partial success).
* **Result:** Alaric strikes the Golem. A standard strike deals 2 damage, but the Golem has **Medium Armor (Armor 2)**. The armor completely blocks the 2 damage. No damage dealt.

##### 3. Pippa's Attack
* **Action:** Pippa darts in to slice at a joint with a throwing knife.
* **Roll:** Dex (+1) + Combat (+0) = +1.
* *Roll:* 6 + 3 = 9 + 1 = **10** (Full Success).
* **Result:** Pippa hits a seam in the bronze pipework. Deals 2 damage. Bypasses 1 armor due to the precise hit seam, so 1 damage is dealt. Golem's Endurance goes from 8 to **7**.

##### 4. Sylvia's Inscription Magic
* **Action:** Sylvia scribes a Glyph of Binding on the Golem's leg.
* **Scope:** Minor (Standard difficulty, +0 modifier).
* **Roll:** Knowledge (+1) + Lore (+1) = +2. Sophia spends a Spark! (Rolls 3d6, keeps top 2).
* *Roll:* 5, 4, 3 (keep 5 and 4) = 9 + 2 = **11** (Full Success).
* **Result:** Frost glyph locks the Golem's right leg. The Golem is **Staggered** and restricted in movement.

##### 5. Thorne's Resonance Magic
* **Action:** Thorne targets the steam valves on the Golem's chest to jam them.
* **Scope:** Minor (Hard difficulty, -1).
* **Roll:** Spirit (+1) + Attune (+1) - 1 (Hard) = +1 modifier. Toby spends his last Spark! (Rolls 3d6, keeps top 2).
* *Roll:* 6, 5, 1 (keep 6 and 5) = 11 + 1 = **12** (Full Success).
* **Result:** Thorne's harmonic vibration jams the steam release valves! The Golem can no longer use its **Steam Blast** technique for the rest of the battle.

### [Simultaneous Exchange 2]

**Cyrus (MM):** The Golem is Staggered, and its steam valves are venting back into its body, making it shudder. However, its phase change activates: the internal steam pressure builds, making its physical strikes deal +1 damage (now 4 damage per hit), and it swings wildly at Pippa.

* **Alaric:** Defensive posture (planning to intercept the hit on Pippa).
* **Pippa:** Measured posture.
* **Sylvia:** Measured posture.
* **Thorne:** Measured posture.

#### [Exchange 2 Resolution]

##### 1. Alaric's Intercept (Defensive Posture)
* **Action:** The Golem swings a lethal stone fist at Pippa (4 damage). Alaric steps in to **Intercept**.
* **Cost:** Intercept normally costs 2 Endurance. In Defensive posture, it costs **1 Endurance**. Alaric's Endurance goes from 3 to **2**.
* **Roll:** Strength (+1) + Combat (+1) = +2.
* *Roll:* 5 + 4 = 9 + 2 = **11** (Full Success).
* **Result:** Alaric successfully intercepts the blow, taking the impact on his shield. The shield rings loudly, but the strike is blocked.

##### 2. Pippa's Luck Strike
* **Action:** While the Golem is focused on Alaric, Pippa slides underneath its stone legs to strike its main core casing.
* **Roll:** Dex (+1) + Combat (+0) = +1 modifier. Penny spends a Spark! (Rolls 3d6, keeps top 2).
* *Roll:* 6, 6, 4 (keep 6 and 6) = 12 + 1 = **13** (Full Success).
* **Result:** A massive hit! Since she got a critical 13, she exploits the Golem's pressurized state. The strike deals 3 damage, completely bypassing its armor as it shatters the stone casing. Golem's Endurance goes from 7 to **4**.

##### 3. Sylvia's Finishing Glyph
* **Action:** Sylvia scribes a glyph of structural collapse on the cracked stone casing.
* **Scope:** Minor (Standard difficulty, +0 modifier).
* **Roll:** Knowledge (+1) + Lore (+1) = +2.
* *Roll:* 5 + 5 = 10 + 2 = **12** (Full Success).
* **Result:** The glyph detonates. The granite chest of the Golem crumbles into dust. The brass gears seize, and the construct collapses into a motionless heap of stone and metal.

**Cyrus (MM):** The Sentry Golem falls. The chamber tremors violently as the glowing Hearth-Core crystal dims slightly, its stabilizing magic released. Water begins pouring in from the cracked ceiling. You have the core, but the spire is collapsing!

> **Alaric:** "Grab the crystal, Thorne! Pippa, run! We need to reach the boat before the entrance is submerged!"
>
> **Thorne:** I grab the warm crystal, wrapping it in my cloak. "I've got it! Let's go!"

**Cyrus (MM):** And that is where we will end today's session! You are running down a crumbling stone corridor with the warm core in hand as freezing lake water rushes in behind you.

**Toby (Thorne):** Wow, that was tense! The Spark system really saved me on that steam valve roll.
**Penny (Pippa):** I lost my sword, but that slide under the legs was amazing! I love how fast combat resolves.
